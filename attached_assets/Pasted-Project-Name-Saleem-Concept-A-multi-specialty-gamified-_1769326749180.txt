Project Name: Saleem (سليم)
Concept: A multi-specialty, gamified patient-doctor portal (iphone app) that transforms medical history and post-op care into an interactive "Quest" while maintaining high Saudi PDPL security standards.support arabic/english 

1. Visual Identity & UI/UX
Colors: Medical Navy (#003366) and Healing Emerald (#50C878).
Font: 'Cairo' (Google Fonts).
Layout: RTL (Arabic) primary, LTR (English) secondary.
The Avatar: A dynamic human silhouette that updates visually as the user adds data.

2. Feature Breakdown (The Build List)
A. Gamified Onboarding (The "Health Hero" Creation)
Explicit Consent: A mandatory, non-pre-ticked popup explaining data usage in KSA.
Avatar Setup: Users input height/weight; the avatar scales accordingly.
The Medicine Cabinet: A drag-and-drop interface where patients place icons of their current meds (Pills, Sprays, Inhalers) into a virtual shelf.
The Badge System: A smart-search bar (using a library of 200+ diseases) where patients select chronic conditions (Diabetes, Hypertension, etc.). These appear as "Badges" on the avatar’s specific organs.
B. Multi-Specialty Interaction (Field by Field)
Modular Content: The app skeleton is the same, but the "Games" and "Instructions" change based on the Doctor’s Specialty Code (ENT, Peds, OBGYN, Optha).
Instruction Sources: All text/games must be derived from NHS (UK) or MedlinePlus (US Gov).
Interactive Games:
ENT: Nasal Wash master (click-to-clean).
Peds: Growth Milestone badges.
OBGYN: Fetal Kick-Tap rhythm tracker.
C. The Medical Vault & OCR
File Uploads: Secure storage for Lab PDFs and Radiology JPGs/DICOMs.
OCR Placeholder: A Python function to "read" uploaded lab screenshots (like from Al Habib app) and suggest "Badges" or "Meds" based on detected text.
Privacy Vault: Patients can "lock" or "unlock" specific files for their doctor to see.
D. Communication Hub
Symptom Body-Map: An SVG silhouette where patients tap an organ (Ear, Eye, Throat) to log a symptom.
Hybrid Chat: A secure messaging room that unlocks after the patient reviews their daily recovery instructions. Includes voice note support.
